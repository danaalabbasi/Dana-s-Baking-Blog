Cinnamon Rolls

WHAT IS IT?

Cinnamon rolls also known as cinnamon buns first originated in Sweden. It is a sweet breakfast/brunch pastry made with a fluffy buttery dough filled with a mixture of cinnamon and brown sugar finished off with a sweet vanilla cream cheese icing. Those irresistible soft and delicate cinnamon rolls are the perfect recipe for weekend mornings or hosting brunch. Here’s everything you need to know about how to make the ideal cinnamon rolls.

Ingredients

FOR THE DOUGH
1 cup of warm whole milk
1 tablespoon of instant dry yeast
1⁄3 cup of granulated sugar
1 egg
1 egg yolk
6 tablespoons of unsalted butter
4 cups of bread flour
1 teaspoon of salt
FOR THE FILLING
6 tablespoons unsalted butter
1 cup of dark brown sugar
2 tablespoons of ground cinnamon
FOR THE ICING
4 ounces of cream cheese
6 tablespoons of unsalted butter
1 cup of powdered sugar
A pinch of salt
1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

STEP 1: MAKE THE DOUGH

Pour warm milk into the bowl of the stand mixer and sprinkle the yeast over it, then add the sugar, melted butter, egg, and egg yolk. Mix everything together then add the flour and salt. Using the dough hook, knead the dough on medium for 8 minutes until a smooth, shiny ball of dough is formed. Place the dough into a lightly greased bowl, cover and let it rest for 60 – 90 minutes until the dough doubled in size.

STEP 2: MAKE THE FILLING

In a small bowl, mix together the melted butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon until everything has been thoroughly combined.

STEP 3: ASSEMBLE THE ROLLS

Transfer the dough to a clean work surface and roll into a 12x18 – inch rectangle. Using a spatula spread the filling all over the surface of the dough leaving ½ inch border. Starting with the short side, roll the dough up tightly into a roulade sealing the edges together. Using a sharp knife, slice the roulade into 12 equal pieces. Place the cinnamon rolls into a baking pan lined with parchment paper. Cover with a kitchen towel and allow to rise till puffy and double in size for 30 – 45 minutes.

STEP 4: BAKE THE ROLLS

Preheat the oven to 350°F/175°C and bake the rolls for about 22 – 27 minutes until lightly golden brown. Remove from oven and set aside to cool.

STEP 5: MAKE THE ICING

In the bowl of an electric mixer, mix together the cream cheese and butter until combined, then add the powdered sugar and vanilla and beat on high until smooth and fluffy. Using a spatula spread the icing over the cinnamon rolls and serve warm.
